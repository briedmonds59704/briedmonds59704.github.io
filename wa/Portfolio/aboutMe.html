Get to know me!

My name is Bri Edmonds. I'm a student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I'm majoring in a division of computer engineering, which is called the Creative Technology and Design (CTD) program. This is my first year in the program, and I'm truly looking forward to learning so much! I'm also a new student ambassador for the CTD program.

Some of my favorite things to do in my free time are reading, baking, hiking, crafting, puzzling, and working out. I love being outside and exploring new places, especially if it involves a good hike! I'm also a big fan of trying out new recipes in the kitchen and experimenting with different ingredients.
